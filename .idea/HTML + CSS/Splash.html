Wars of the Rogue

What is Wars of the Rogue?

It is a strategy game you can play in your browser, requiring no download.

You must build up a base while repelling enemy attacks. You must then send your units to attack and destroy the enemy commander in order to win.

Play the Game

Map Summary

Forgotten Islands

Twin islands created by volcanic disruption in the ocean. The deep water prevents any land units from making their way between the islands.

Desert Storm

A land of sand that prevented military invasions for the land of the east for centuries. Due to newer technologies, this desert no longer means trouble for invading armies.

Green Plains

Formerly a mining site that was then converted to a nature reserve. The land rights were soon sold and a logging firm removed much of the forest, with the animals moved to other reserves or zoos.

Building Summary

ዧ

Metal Extractor

The Metal Extractor is required in order to increase your metal production each turn. This can only be built on metal zones.

ቸ

Power Plant

The Power Plant is required in order to increase your power production each turn. This building can only be built on land.

ጁ

Land Factory

The Land Factory is used to construct land units. This building can only be built on land.

ᎇ

Naval Factory

The Naval Factory is used to construct naval units. This building can only be built in the sea.

ኡ

Turret

The Turret can be used to attack enemy units and buildings within a short radius. This building can only be built on land.

ፏ

Heavy Artillery

The Heavy Artillery can be used to attack enemy units and buildings anywhere on the map. It requires a turn to reload after firing. This building can only be built on land.

Unit Summary - Land Units

ደ

Command Unit

The Command Unit is the only unit that can construct buildings. It can also travel on land or in shallow sea.

Ⱝ

Tank

The Tank does light damage in a short radius and can only travel on land.

Ѫ

Lab Droid

The Lab Droid does heavier damage in a larger radius. It can also travel on land but also on metal zones.

ᛣ

Missile Carrier

The Missile Carrier does a lot of damage in a large radius but requires a turn to reload its missiles. It can only travel on land.

ᵹ

Detonating Sphere

The Detonating Sphere is the most damaging unit but has a tiny radius. Once it attacks, it is destroyed. It can only travel on land.

Unit Summary - Naval Units

₣

Destroyer

The Destroyer is the least damaging naval unit and can only attack in a short radius. It can only travel in the sea.

₸

Cruiser

The Cruiser does increased damage but over a much larger radius. It can only travel in the sea.

₮

Battleship

The Battleship does a lot of damage across a large radius but requires a turn to reload its ammunition. It can only travel in the sea.
